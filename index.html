Overview

PhysEditWorld studies whether world models can respond to physical rules as editable parameters. Each matched replay group keeps the scene, initial state, action trace, controller, and camera policy fixed while changing gravity.

The dataset exports synchronized multi-view videos and engine-side metadata, making physical differences directly inspectable across counterfactual rollouts.
